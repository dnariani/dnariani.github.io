Project Name: Accounting Firm - Specter Accounting & Co.

Project Overview

Describe Application and Its Purpose

This website represents a fictional accounting firm named Specter Accounting & Co., inspired by Harvey Specter. It is an elite accounting firm catering to high-value clients. The design and content are crafted to exude luxury, confidence, and exclusivity, positioning the firm as the ultimate choice for corporations, business moguls, and individuals seeking top-tier financial solutions with unmatched professionalism.

Intended Users of the Website

  • Future and Potential Clients
  • Corporations with accounting needs
  • People who own businesses
  • People who want information about the accounting firm

Site Map

Page Design

Home - What

Purpose: To introduce people to the firm and highlight what it is.

User/Audience: Potential clients who are interested in our firm.

Content: Slogan, Photo that represents the company or office, mini introduction

About Us - Who

Purpose: To explain what the firm is, introduce its mission, values, and history.

User/Audience: Potential clients who want to see the background of the firm and what its about.

Content: A description of the firm's history, info, values, mission.

Services - When

Purpose: To outline the firm's service offerings, helping clients understand when they might need the firm's expertise.

User/Audience: Potential clients assessing service options.

Content: Sections for different types of services the firm offers.

Testimonials - Why

Purpose: Show former clients who liked our services to create trust with the firm.

User/Audience: Potential clients who want insights into varied experiences.

Content: Fictional testimonials with client names, titles, and comments and possibly success stories.

Contact Us - Where

Purpose: Provide users with the ability to send inquiries or book consultations.

User/Audience: People who are interested in the firm and want information and potential clients seeking assistance.

Content:

    Message/Info Form:
    • Input Name, Email, Subject, Message.
    • Required input for Name, Email, Subject, and Message.
    • There is a Submit button to send the info.
    Booking Consultation Form:
    • Input Name, Email, Phone Number, Date, Time, Type of Consultation.
    • Make sure inputs are correctly formatted and required.
    • Submit button allow the appointment to get created.

Dynamic Functionality on the Website:

If you click the contact button it will by deflaut lead you to the message form.
